The data that will be presented on this beautiful website is from the Department of Health and Mental Hygiene (DOHMH). Their website includes a BUNCH of the good stuff, like information on how to find child care, emergency preparedness, health insurance, and in this case, BABY NAMES.

The dataset used by your amazing classmates Asael and Adrian was Popular Baby Names. You can find many baby names on this page by only entering an ethnicity of your choice. This beautiful data can be used to represent the popularity of a name. So instead of having to look up a baby name, you can just look at the data shown on this website for inspiration.
